Generating realistic-looking NYUAD behavior

NYUAD students go through several problems that affect their day to day. Whether it is the shock of seeing a beautiful person sitting across from them in D2, the ever-increasing batch of grad school and job applications, a new controversial rumor jumping from ear to ear, or a global pandemic, NYUAD students have relied on online platforms like NYUAD Confessions and NYUAD Crushes and Compliments to express their viewpoints/emotions/dissapointments about the world around them.

Using Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing, we were able to train a model using Confessions and Crushes and Compliments data to generate text that sounds like an NYUAD student.
